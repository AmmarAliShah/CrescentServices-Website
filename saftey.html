Safety

Our strength and reputation is based on the hard work and professionalism of our employees. It is absolutely important for us that our employees remain happy and satisfied at all times with the environments they work in and have to move around in. to this end, Crescent Services pays special attention to details and the health of all its staff.
Crescent Services ensures the complete safety and security of its own employees and that of the general public and that no one is harmed because of our services.
We take great measures to overcome any and all hazards that may arise due to transport and moving of any load. These include but are not limited to:
Vehicles: whether they are maintained, safe, and suitable for use.
Routes: whether they are obstacle free, maintained and suitable for our vehicles, or are there any other options available.
Employees: Our drivers, their schedule, time constraints, risks and safety precautions and proper loading and unloading.
All drivers and field staff members are in constant communication with our offices at all times, providing up to date information for all routes and works, limiting hazards and risks to an absolute minimum.
