Contracting

Contracting involves the execution of complicated lifting and transport operations, whereby finding the right execution methods as the supporting activity is vital. A contract package typically includes engineering, planning, execution and management of our heavy lift and transport activities within agreed parameters. Customers increasingly choose for modular construction of refineries, oil platforms and other industrial installations and are specifying increasingly heavy and large components. The design limitations on size and weight are primarily determined by the available handling capacity; boundaries which Crescent Services is continuing to push back. An essential aspect of building in large modules is that the total construction and contract time will be reduced.
