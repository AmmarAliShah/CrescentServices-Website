Transportation

Crescent Services has the experience and capability to find the best solutions in transporting a wide range of structures, equipment, modules, windmill project equipment & oversized cargo. Crescent Services manages the entire operation from the selection of equipment to loading, securing, unloading activities; determination of jurisdictional weight and size restrictions; relocation of utilities and obstacles. The company has developed excellent long-standing mutually respectful relations with regulatory and advisory agencies governing transportation. These relationships enable us to provide the best transportation solutions for our customers.

  • Transportation on turnkey basis
  • Transportation of oversized cargo
  • Transportation of General & bulk cargo
  • Custom Clearance
  • Special & precise equipment for loading & hauling
