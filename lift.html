Heavy Lifting

Crescent Services has the expertise and the equipment to offer complete lifting solutions from conventional to highly innovative. Every lift is engineered and planned in detail and includes an engineered rigging study to ensure maximum safety and efficiency throughout the entire lifting procedure. We offer the advanced and extensive inventory of lifting equipment in the Pakistan, including cranes up to 600 tons capacity, jacking systems and a variety of versatile lifting accessories. Lift accessories are frequently designed and constructed to meet the specific requirements of a particular lift. All rigging equipment is regularly maintained and inspected to the highest standards and key components are further load-tested to ensure the utmost lifting safety.

  • Lifting of heavy & oversized cargo
  • Discharging of heavy Cargo from Vessel
  • Lowering of heavy Cargo in Sea
  • Replacing of Modules, wind blades & Transformers etc.
  • Rental Cranes
